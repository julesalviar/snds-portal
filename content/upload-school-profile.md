**Private entities often need detailed information about schools they're considering for adoption or support as part of their corporate social responsibility initiatives. A school profile is one of the most important documents they'll want to review, as it provides a comprehensive overview of the school's history and current situation. This profile serves as a basis for their selection process during board meetings. Therefore, it's crucial to prepare a downloadable school profile that gives them a solid understanding of our school before they even visit.**

### Process steps in uploading School Profile.

1. Go to your school account.
2. Click logout button and select “edit profile”.
3. Scroll down to the bottom of the screen
4. Drag and drop prepared school profile in PDF format, or click to browse the 
school profile.
5. Click “update profile” button
6. End of the process.

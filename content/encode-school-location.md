**Recognizing that locating our public schools can be a challenge for stakeholders, we are committed to providing an intuitive, one-click solution by using school coordinates. This will significantly improve accessibility and encourage increased engagement with our schools**<br>

### What are the specific steps for extracting school coordinates accurately using Google Maps?  
**Method 1: Using Right-Click (Desktop)**
1. **Open Google Maps:** Go to https://www.google.com/maps in a web browser.<br>
2. **Find the School:** Search for the specific school by name or address.<br>
3. **Pinpoint the Location:** Ensure the school is visible on the map. Zoom in if necessary to 
precisely locate the school building or campus.<br>
4. **Right-Click:** Right-click directly on the school's icon or building on the map. A context menu 
will appear.<br>
5. **Copy the Coordinates:** The coordinates (latitude and longitude) will be displayed at the top of 
the menu in decimal degrees format. Click on the coordinates to copy them.<br>
6. **Paste:** Paste the copied coordinates into your desired document and paste it in the "location" 
box.<br>

**Method 1: Using Long-Press (Mobile)**
1. **Open Google Maps:** Open the Google Maps app on your mobile device.
2. **Find the School:** Search for the specific school by name or address.
3. **Pinpoint the Location:** Ensure the school is visible on the map. Zoom in if necessary to 
precisely locate the school building or campus.
4. **Long-Press:** Tap and hold (long-press) on the school's location until a red pin appears.
5. **Copy the Coordinates:** After the pin drops, the coordinates will appear in the search bar or 
within an info panel at the bottom of the screen. Tap the coordinates to copy them.
6. **Paste:** Paste the copied coordinates into your desired document and paste it in the "location" 
box.<br>

**Method 2: Using the "What's Here?" Option (Desktop)**
1. **Open Google Maps:** Go to https://www.google.com/maps in a web browser.
2. **Find the School:** Search for the specific school.
3. **Click on the School:** Click directly on the school's icon or building on the map.
4. **"What's Here?":** A small information panel will appear at the bottom of the screen. Look for a 
"What's here?" link or text within the panel and click on it.
5. **Copy Coordinates:** The coordinates (latitude and longitude) will be displayed in the 
information panel. Copy them.


### Process steps in uploading School Profile.
**1.** Go to your school account.<br>
**2.** Click logout button and select “edit profile”.<br>
**3.** Scroll down to and select “location” box.<br>
**4.** Paste the copied coordinates into your desired document and paste it in the "location" 
box.<br>
**5.** Click “update profile” button<br>
**6.** End of the process.
